Adam Thorpe

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the ODEN Institute for Computational Engineering & Science at the University of Texas at Austin. I received my Ph.D. in 2023 from the University of New Mexico. I am broadly interested in topics related to data-driven methods for control, computational engineering, and human-centered autonomy.
